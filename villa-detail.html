Situated in the middle of Ubud’s best views, Villa Atap Padi offers more than just beautiful landscapes and tranquility. Once you step in, a alluring home-feelings enters the minds by looking over comfortable open space living rooms facing straight to the swimming pool. The infinity pool teases anyone to jump dive in and bathe under the sun overlooking magnificent rice field views and jungles.

Enjoy a serene morning by sipping a cup of coffee or tea at the pool bar while listening the birds singing. Have a delicious merry meal over at our wooden dining table, which serving Indonesian delicacies for lunch or dinner. When you are tired, step down to the riverside and enjoy a relaxing spa treatment for couples with our profesional spa therapists.

Get the best out of Ubud by taking a bike ride to Tegallalang famous rice terrace, taking a instagram shot at the famous Bali’s Swing, or have a cultural adventure at Antonio Blanco’s Museum. Strategicaly placed among these attraction spots surely gives another values for staying at Villa Atap Padi, both for family holiday or honeymoon destination for newlyweds.

The master bedroom is equipped with a huge closet, comfortable bed, TV with international channels, and spacious bathroom with large bathub. There are two other bedrooms, all equipped with king size bed and air conditioner, TV, and bathroom in each rooms.

Sleep peacefully at another two spacious bedrooms; which elegantly furnished with handmade furniture include luxury en-suite facilities with complimentary amenities pack, flat screen LCD TV, air conditioner, fan, hairdryer and the finest pure white linen and towels. All the rooms are filled with alluring natural lights and the view might make it hard for you to leave the room.

For extra guests, Villa Atap Padi offer one more bedroom; with extra privacy and located separately with the main villa. This ensure the condition in which you can still be comfortable and have the privacy while your friends are exactly next door.

The parking area can cater up to 3 4-wheeled vehicle, means that you are able to invite a larger number of family member or friends to enjoy wine night, with maximum number of 20 person to enjoy dining on the pooldeck. Our staff is on standby ready to pamper your holiday for 24 hours.
